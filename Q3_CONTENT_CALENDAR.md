## **RISK MITIGATION**

**What if we get behind on content?**
- Prioritize authority guides (SEO impact)
- Blog posts can be shorter (800 words minimum)
- Combine case studies with blog format (1 piece instead of 2)
- Extend timeline to October if needed

**What if performance is poor?**
- Mid-August review: Adjust topics if underperforming
- Focus on high-performing keywords
- Double down on comparison pages (high intent)
- Increase promotion budget for top pieces

**What if team capacity is limited?**
- Reduce blog posts to 2/week instead of 3
- Focus on authority guides (higher ROI per hour)
- Hire freelance writer for 2-3 blog posts/week
- Consider repurposing existing content (interviews, webinars)
