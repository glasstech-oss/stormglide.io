# StormGlide Backlink Outreach Strategy
## Target: 10-20 High-Quality Backlinks in Q3 2026

---

## **STRATEGY OVERVIEW**

**Goal**: Build high-quality backlinks from authoritative African tech, business, and startup sites.

**Why Backlinks Matter**:
- Single biggest SEO ranking factor
- Authority guide content is "link-worthy" (attracts backlinks)
- African tech community actively shares content
- 10-20 quality links = 30-40 position improvement in rankings

**Outreach Approach**:
1. **Guest Posts** (5-8 links) — Write for relevant publications
2. **Resource Links** (5-10 links) — Get featured in "best guides" lists
3. **Partnerships** (2-3 links) — Co-marketing with complementary companies
4. **HARO/Media** (2-3 links) — Respond to journalist queries
5. **Community Mentions** (3-5 links) — Featured in newsletters, podcasts

**Target**: 10-20 links by Sept 30, 2026
